Images

This page covers common image formats, but also check out links and some extras.

JPGs:

JPG images are good for photos.
They handle large color palattes well, but can't have transparent pixels.

GIFs:

GIFs are good for animations!
They can handle transparency, but not partial opacity. They have less dynamic colors than JPGs.

PNGs:

PNGS are good for diagrams and icons.
They handle multiple levels of opacity, and can have as many colors as a JPG, but typically have larger file sizes.

SVGs:

SVGs are vector-based graphics, so they can scale to any size.
This makes them very good for responsive websites.
